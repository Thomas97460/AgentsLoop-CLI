# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and inclusive.
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement
Maintainers have the right to remove content or ban participants not following these standards. Issues can be reported privately to the project maintainers.
